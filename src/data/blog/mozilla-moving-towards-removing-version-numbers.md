Following the lead of Chrome, Mozilla is working towards burying versionnumbers. ghacks sniffed this out in a Bugzilla ticket. See the [ghacks
article](http://www.ghacks.net/2011/08/14/mozilla-plans-to-hide-firefox-version/) for a brief walkthrough, and check out the [Bugzilla ticket](https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=678775)
for the full run-down.
